Brief Biography

Sule Solace Adamu was born in Azare town, Hawul Local Government Area of Borno State. He attended Agbassa Primary School Warri, Essi Junior College III Warri, and Federal Government College Warri for his primary, junior and senior secondary education respectively.

He is currently a fourth-year student at the University of Maiduguri pursuing a Bachelor of Engineering (B.Eng.) Degree in the field of Computer Engineering.

He meritoriously served as the Assistant Auditor General of Computer Engineering Students Association (CPESA), UniMaid Chapter, during the 2019/2020 academic session showing exemplary leadership qualities, integrity, moral and intellectual advancement.

S.S.Adamu is a Front-End Developer, Data Analyst and a Crypto Enthusiast. His hobbies include cooking, reading, swimming and music.
